2. Close-up — what to look for

3. What they look like in your hand

  • Shape: tiny square plastic button, about 4–6 mm, sitting flush or slightly above the green PCB.
  • Feel: a soft click when pressed (tactile switch).
  • Labels: printed on the board in small letters: BOOT and RESET (or EN on some boards — yours should say RESET).
  • Location: on the same side of the board as the USB-C port, not on the speaker/mic end.
Download mode for erase:
  1. Hold BOOT down.
  2. Tap RESET once (press and release).
  3. Release BOOT.
  4. Within 3 seconds, run python3 -m esptool ... erase_flash in Terminal.
