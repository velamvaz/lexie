Revised clip design: Replace the slot cutout (between-pages) with a PETG C-spring jaw at the top of the device body. The jaw gap at rest is ~4mm; PETG's natural flex lets it grip book covers 2–12mm thick without any metal spring. The lower jaw is a flat tab that goes behind the front cover; the upper jaw is the back of the device. The child simply hooks it onto the top-right corner of the book — same motion as clipping a reading light.
What changes in the enclosure design:
Remove the slot cutout at the top. Replace with a solid C-shaped overhang that extends 8–10mm behind the device body, creating a spring jaw. The jaw mouth faces downward so the child clips it onto the book from above. Print in PETG at 1.0mm wall thickness for jaw arms — this gives enough flex without being floppy.
